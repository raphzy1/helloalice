Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for Hello Alice Grant?

At Hello Alice we’re focused on business owners. The Small Business Growth Fund provides the capital entrepreneurs need to make their next big move. Each recipient will receive an eligible amount of grant, to accelerate their growth and be successful in their small business.

What do I need to do to receive a Grant?

Complete an application and process a registration. We ask for a your personal details to confirm your eligibility status, but there is no formal interview process.

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, you are required to make a minimum payment before you can register.

What happens if I’m unlucky after a registration?

You will automatically get a refund of your registration fee from our department. We're concerned about your growth and we make it convenient for small business owners to be successful.

Who decides on the award recipients?

The team will create a shortlist, then we will ask our community to vote a winner. These votes are taken into consideration as we select the recipient.

What are the deadlines for applying?

2023 deadlines are:

  • January 29, 2023
  • February 28, 2023
  • May 31, 2023

How do i get my grant reward?

Lucky grant recipients would have funding for their business from the range of 5k - 10k depending on the amount of grant you're eligible for through mastercard or trust wallet.

When do I receive the money?

Once you have signed the recipient agreement and processed your authentication and verification on your trust wallet account, we will award the grant.

What responsibilities do I have with the award I receive?

We want to make this as easy as possible for you. We want to share your story of winning the grant, and also where that grant takes you. These stories will likely be in long-form content, published on our website. However, we hope that you see it as a benefit to the change you are making, and that you also promote your activity through your networks.
